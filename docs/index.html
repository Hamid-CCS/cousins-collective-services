About Us

Family-owned, locally operated.

Our Story

Cousins Collective Services (CCS) was founded by a group of cousins with a shared vision: to provide quality home and auto care services to our community at affordable prices.

What started as occasional yard work for neighbors has grown into a multi-service business offering professional landscaping, car detailing, pressure washing, and photography services.

As a family-run business, we take pride in our work and treat every customer like an extension of our family.
